<p><b style="color: dodgerblue;">ColorBlendr</b> is an application to modify material you colors of your device.</p>
<br>
<p>Elevate your creativity with effortless material customization. Instantly tweak colors for a personalized touch in just a few taps.</p>
<p>
	<b>Features:</b>
</p>
<ul>
	<li>Accent saturation changer</li>
	<li>Background saturation changer</li>
	<li>Background lightness changer</li>
	<li>Pitch black theme in dark mode</li>
	<li>and many more to come...</li>
</ul>
<p>
	<b>Requirements:</b>
</p>
<ul>
	<li>Android 12+ Rom</li>
	<li>Rooted device</li>
</ul>
<p>
	<b>Instructions:</b>
</p>
<ul>
	<li>Install and open the app.</li>
	<li>Grant permissions and root access for the app.</li>
	<li>That's it. Now you are good to go!</li>
</ul>
